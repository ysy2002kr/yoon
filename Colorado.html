Yoon's Colorado Adventure

Colorad has it's own vibe. It almost feels like a town in Europe. Surronded by the beautiful natures, Colorado is one of the nation's go-to place for skiing and winter sports.

One thing to be careful in Denver, Colorado is the high elevation level causing health problems to kids or seniors. Do not perform any vigorous activities during your first or second day. Drinking a lot of water and engaging in less dynamic activity to adjust the elevation level is recommended.

For nature lovers, it will be a great place to hike and explore national parks.
