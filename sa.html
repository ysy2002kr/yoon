South America was the most beautiful continent I have ever visited. The nature, the people, the culture, everything made my trip in South America very special.

If you are worried about safety, I recommend starting in Argentina or Chile. You always need to take precautions, but these two countries are wealthier than most of other countries in South America and thus less threat to the travelers.

Aside from the safety issues, South America is defenitely worth visit.
